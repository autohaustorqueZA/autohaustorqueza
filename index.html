Eco-Friendly Promise

We Care About The Bay

We care about the roads and the environment around Nelson Mandela Bay. All used oils, fluids, batteries, and parts are collected and handed to licensed South African waste handlers and recyclers — never dumped or burned.

Sustainable practices are baked into every job because a healthier planet means better drives for our children and future Mzansi generations. "Inzala yethu iyalinda." (Our children are watching.)

🛢️

Oil & Fluid Disposal

Used oils and coolants are collected and handed to licensed SA recyclers. Never dumped.

🔋

Battery Recycling

Old batteries handed to certified recycling partners — lead-acid recycling that doesn't harm the Bay.

♻️

Parts & Waste

Old parts and workshop waste sorted and directed to approved SA waste management facilities.

🌿

Clean Work Sites

Your driveway is left spotless. We carry clean-up materials on every job. No spills, no mess, no trace.
